-About World conected-

The world connected Gest House
The number of foreigners visiting Japan for tourism, study abroad, and technical training is rapidly increasing by globalization.
Excluding the impact of the new coronavirus

Under these circumstances, the acceptance of foreigners by the Japanese people has not progressed
due to differences in language, religion, culture, customs, etc.
So various human rights issues have arisen.

From the Cabinet Office “Public opinion poll on human rights protection” (August 2020 survey): Human rights issues related to foreigners

World connected GH. serves as a bridge between foreigners and Japan, contributing to a multicultural society.
To reduce prejudice and discrimination against foreigners and to recognize and respect each other's culture.
