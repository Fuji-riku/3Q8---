Local international exchange events

-Event-

It is currently difficult for foreigners visiting Japan to belong to local communities in Japan. World connected GH. will serve as a bridge between local residents and guest house residents by holding various cultural exchange events and creating opportunities for interaction between guest house residents and residents of the surrounding area.

International food festival

An event where local residents and guesthouse
residents share traditional dishes from their
respective countries and regions. By cooking
and eating together, you can deepen your
understanding of each other.

Japanese history walking tour

Japanese guest house residents and local
residents will act as guides and tour
tourist spots and unique local places.
We will create opportunities for
residents from overseas to enjoy Japan.

International movie viewing party

Residents can bring their own films and
screen them inside the guesthouse,
allowing visitors to deepen their
understanding of other countries
by transcending culture, history,
and language.

Participating in local festivals

Guesthouse residents are invited to
participate in traditional festivals
and events in the surrounding area to
learn about traditional Japanese
culture and build relationships with
local people.
