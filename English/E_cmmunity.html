Community building

-コミュニティ形成-

At World conected GH., in order to promote communication within the guesthouse, we use social media such as Slack and LINE to form a community and provide an environment where people can share their interests and goals.

Thematic groups

We have created an environment on social media
where residents can create thematic groups with
common hobbies and goals, allowing them to
enjoy their hobbies together within the
guesthouse and find friends to collaborate
with towards their goals.

Weekly topic discussion

We widen the circle of interaction within the
guest house by holding discussions on different
topics every week, with voluntary participation,
and by setting up discussion groups where
participants can freely exchange opinions.

Challenge series

We propose challenges based on monthly themes
and provide opportunities for participants to
take on the challenges. It provides a place to
share progress and experiences, increases
motivation for challenges, and helps
participants communicate with each other.
